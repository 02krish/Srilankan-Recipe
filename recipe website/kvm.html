Konda kavum

Ingredients:

1/4 cup grated coconut (fresh or desiccated)
1/2 cup jaggery (or brown sugar)
1/4 teaspoon salt
1 cup water
Oil for frying

Instructions:

1. -Heat oil in a deep frying pan on medium heat.

2. -Once the oil is hot, take a kavum mold (a special mold used for making kavum) or a small round spoon.

3. -Dip the mold/spoon into the hot oil for a few seconds to heat it.

4. -Dip the heated mold/spoon into the prepared batter, making sure it coats the bottom and sides of the mold.

5. -Carefully place the batter-coated mold/spoon into the hot oil, allowing the batter to slide off into the oil.

6. -Repeat this process to make multiple kavum in the frying pan, but do not overcrowd the pan.

7. -Fry the kavum on medium heat, turning occasionally, until they turn golden brown and crispy on all sides.

8. -Once done, remove the fried kavum using a slotted spoon and place them on paper towels to absorb excess oil.

9. -Repeat the process with the remaining batter, ensuring the oil is at the right temperature before making each batch.

10. -Allow the kavum to cool before serving. They can be stored in an airtight container for a few days
